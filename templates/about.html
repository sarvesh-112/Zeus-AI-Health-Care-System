About Zeus Hospital

Revolutionizing Healthcare with AI | Certify.o Open Source Hackathon

Our Story

Zeus Hospital is an AI-powered healthcare platform developed by Sarvesh Ganesan as a third-year engineering project. It was showcased at the prestigious Open Source Hackathon conducted by Certify.o. Built to modernize health diagnostics, Zeus Hospital is driven by the mission to make AI accessible, fast, and friendly for medical use.

Vision

To democratize healthcare through intelligent, AI-driven systems that help individuals understand and take charge of their health using intuitive digital tools.

Mission

Our mission is to develop a cutting-edge web app that blends machine learning, design, and usability to assist users in predicting diseases based on symptoms and to guide them toward healthier choices through education and automation.
